welcom to zaytoonah university

Al-Zaytoonah University of Jordan (Arabic جامعة الزيتونة الأردنية), founded in 1993, is a private university located in Amman, Jordan. It is accredited by the Jordanian Ministry of Higher Education & Scientific Research. As of 2024 there were 8,663 students enrolled in the university of whom 2,652 were international students.[1] As is the case in all other Jordanian universities, the credit-hour system is used in the university. google
